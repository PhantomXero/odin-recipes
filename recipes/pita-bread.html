You gotta try this easy and oh boy delicious chicken pita sandwich recipe!

Elevate your taste experience with our mouthwatering Grilled BBQ Chicken Pita! Embrace the best of both worlds as succulent, smoky BBQ chicken meets the warm embrace of soft, fluffy pita bread. Bursting with a harmonious blend of Mediterranean spices and marinated to perfection, this culinary delight promises to tantalize your senses. Embark on a flavor adventure as you sink your teeth into the juicy chicken, accompanied by fresh crisp veggies and a drizzle of creamy tahini sauce. One bite, and you'll be transported to a realm of irresistible taste sensations!

ingredients

  • 1/2 Small red onion sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup garbanzo beans from a can
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice more for drizzing on top
  • 1 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 2 medium slices tomatoes
  • 1 small avocado

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine all of the ingredients inside the bowl, marinate over the night or at least 1 hour. Grill on high heat for 6-8 minutes on each side.
  2. In a mixing bowl combine onion, garbanzo beans, olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Grill pita bread then spread yogurt, add arugula, tomatoes, garbanzo bean mixture, avocado and grilled chicken slices. Serve with greek yogurt, a squeeze of lemon juice and a sprinkle of black pepper over the top.
  4. Enjoy delicious homemade Grilled Chicken Pita Sandwich!
